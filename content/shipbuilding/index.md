Due to a variety of ongoing issues, sales of OOG ships is now suspended, effective immediately.  This is due to a number of reasons:
* Very high internal and external demand for ships
* Internal changes to ship component production (old shipbuilders stopping, and getting new bases online)
* A rise in market prices, such that some of our ships are selling at a loss
* The ongoing political situation regarding Promitor

We will let you know when ship sales resume. We will have a slightly different model with different people handling the frontend vs. backend of ship sales, so I don't burn out so badly trying to manage it all.

My apologies to everyone who has requested a ship recently. We will not be able to fulfill them in a timely manner, and I will be closing / resetting the queue.
